Our History & Vision

A legacy of educational leadership in the Heart of Cambodia.

Cultivating the Next Generation of Khmer Leaders

Founded in 2010, Vattanak Premier Academy was established with a singular vision: to bridge the gap between traditional Cambodian excellence and global innovation. We believe that every student in Cambodia deserves a world-class education that respects our rich heritage while embracing the future.

Our Mission

To provide a transformative bilingual education that develops critical thinkers, compassionate leaders, and lifelong learners.

Our Vision

To be the premier center for secondary education in SE Asia, known for academic rigor and cultural integrity.
